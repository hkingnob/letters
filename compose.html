Write a letter

Attach a file — a photo of a note, a document, anything. Once posted, it can't be unsent.

Travel time depends on the distance between you: same city about 2 days, same country about 4, same continent about 7, overseas about 12.

Posting reads your device's location for the postmark and to work out how long it travels. You'll be asked to allow location — it's required.
